BEST TIME TO VISIT

Kerala is best when explored in the winter season. The summers in Kerala are too hot for comfort and hence it is best to be avoided. Also, the main monsoons keep the state wet all throughout the season. Although it can be a beautiful amalgamation of scenic views, it becomes difficult to explore outdoor activities during the monsoons. So the best time definitely is during the winter season. One can enjoy adventure activities, go boating and trekking and all this while being comfortable.
